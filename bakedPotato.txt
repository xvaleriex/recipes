Ingredients
350 g Potato(es), Raw, 2, baking
300 g Mushrooms, cooked and sliced
50 g Medium fat soft cheese
1 teaspoons Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 teaspoons Black pepper
Instructions
Cook the baking potatoes for 10-12 minutes in the microwave or for 1-11 hours in a conventional oven at Gas Mark 6/200°C/400°F.

Mix the mushrooms with the soft cheese and season with the Worcestershire sauce and black pepper.

Slit open the jacket potatoes and top with the cheese mixture to serve.
